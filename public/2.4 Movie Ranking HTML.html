A Few of My Favorite Films

Top 4 Movies, In No Particular Order

Fury

This is a great war film that really shows the cunning, stress, and horrors of living & working in a tank during WWII.

Talladega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby

This is a classic comedy IMO. It combines my favorite comedic duo with NASCAR, what's not to like?

Dodgeball

Another great comedy movie from my childhood. I really connect with this film because of the underdog storyline and my love for dodgeball.

Ironman

This is my all time favorite superhero movie. This is the film that got me interested in the Marvel Universe. Plus Robert Downey Jr. is a straight up G.
